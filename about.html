Oahu

Welcome to "The Gathering Place". This is where most of the population lives. Visitors will find a mixture of modern arts and entertainment on this island. Soak up the sun at Waikiki. Visit the museums of Pearl Harbor or see the surfers challenge the big waves of the North Shore.

Maui

Aloha, if you are looking for some of the best beaches in the world, look no further. This is also one of the best places to whale watch in the winter. This "Valley Island" is known for stunning scenary, sleepy towns, and world class resorts.

Hawaii Island

Also known as the Big Island. The largest of the islands. Famous for waterfalls, rainforests and botanical gardens. Home to the Kono coffee plantation.

Kauai

Known as "The Garden Island" it is blessed with dramatic scenary. See Waimea Canyon AKA "the Grand Canyon of the Pacific". Visit the towering sea cliffs along the Napali Coast.

Molokai

Tired of the fast pace of modern life, step back in time to this peaceful island. Known as "The Friendly Island you won't find any chain stores, tall buildings, or night clubs here.

Lanai

Ever been to a place without a single traffic light? Looking for untouched natural beauty and unpaved roads? This is the place. Visit Hulopoe Bay named one of "America's Best Beaches". Stop by Kehiakawelo aka the "Garden of the Gods" and see the rock formations.
