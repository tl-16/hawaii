Pearl Harbor Tour

Take this journey through US history with stops at the Pearl Harbor Visitor Center, the USS Arizona Memorial, and a guided tour of the USS Missouri.

Paradise Cove Luau

Don't miss one of the most repected luau shows in Hawaii. The fun begins at sunset with a Mai Tai greeting. Listen to live Hawaiian music while strolling through the Hawaiian village. Watch the Royal Court Procession and enjoy a sumptuous feast.

Oahu Shark Dive

Enjoy a thrilling encounter with sharks up close and personal. Depart on a relaxing 3 mile cruise where you may see turtles and dolphins. Step inside the secure shark cage for a 30 minute shark-viewing adventure. Don't forget to bring your camera!

Volcanoes and Waterfalls Helicopter Adventure Tour

See the best of Hawaii from the air.Venture over miles of tropical rainforest and past hidden waterfalls. See Kilauea, one of the most active volcanoes on the planet. Watch lava flow from the crater. You'll get incredible aerial views of the wonders that make Hawaii so great.
